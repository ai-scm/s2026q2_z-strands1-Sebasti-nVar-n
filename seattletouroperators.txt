# Seattle Tour Operators Directory

## Water Activities Tour Operators

### Puget Sound Express
- **Specialty:** Whale watching and marine wildlife tours
- **Location:** Pier 69, Seattle Waterfront
- **Services:** 3-4 hour whale watching excursions, private charters
- **Season:** Year-round operations with peak season May-October
- **Highlights:** Orca whales, harbor seals, sea lions, and diverse marine  birds
- **Contact:** (206) 555-WHALE
- **Website:** pugetsoundexpress.com

### Seattle Harbor Tours
- **Specialty:** Harbor cruises and city skyline tours
- **Location:** Pier 55/56, Seattle Waterfront
- **Services:** 1-hour harbor tours, 2.5-hour locks tour, dinner cruises
- **Season:** Daily departures year-round
- **Highlights:** Elliott Bay, Seattle skyline, Ballard Locks, maritime  history
- **Contact:** (206) 555-HARBOR
- **Website:** seattleharbortours.com

### Argosy Cruises
- **Specialty:** Sightseeing and special event cruises
- **Location:** Multiple piers - Pier 55, Pier 56
- **Services:** Lake Washington tours, Lake Union tours, holiday cruises
- **Season:** Year-round with seasonal variations
- **Highlights:** Floating bridges, houseboats, seaplane activity, seasonal events
- **Contact:** (206) 555-ARGOSY
- **Website:** argosycruises.com

## Land-Based Tour Operators

### Seattle Food Tours
- **Specialty:** Culinary walking tours
- **Location:** Various neighborhoods - Pike Place, Capitol Hill, Fremont
- **Services:** 3-hour food walking tours, private group tours
- **Season:** Year-round operations
- **Highlights:** Local restaurants, Pike Place Market, craft breweries, coffee culture
- **Contact:** (206) 555-FOOD
- **Website:** seattlefoodtours.com

### Underground Tour Company
- **Specialty:** Historical underground Seattle tours
- **Location:** Pioneer Square area
- **Services:** 75-minute guided underground tours, ghost tours
- **Season:** Daily tours year-round
- **Highlights:** 1890s underground passages, Seattle history, architecture
- **Contact:** (206) 555-UNDER
- **Website:** undergroundtour.com

## Adventure Tour Operators

### Seattle Bike Tours
- **Specialty:** Bicycle tours and rentals
- **Location:** Multiple locations - Waterfront, Fremont, University District
- **Services:** 2.5-hour city bike tours, bike rentals, custom tours
- **Season:** Year-round with weather considerations
- **Highlights:** Burke-Gilman Trail, waterfront paths, neighborhood    exploration
- **Contact:** (206) 555-BIKE
- **Website:** seattlebiketours.com

## Certification and Safety Information

All tour operators listed are vetted and approved by Seattle Tourism Board and maintain current insurance and safety certifications.
